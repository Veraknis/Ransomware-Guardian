"What is Ransomware Guardian?":

Ransomware Guardian is a new platform designed to provide fast, accurate, and real-time tracking of ransomware activities.

By leveraging advanced data aggregation, expert analysis, and multiple API sources, we deliver unparalleled insights into emerging ransomware threats.

"Why is Ransomware Guardian Needed?"

In 2022, there were approximately 493.3 million ransomware attacks.

Ransomware attacks increased by over 37% in 2023, with the average enterprise ransom payment exceeding $100,000 with a $5.3 million average demand.
There are currently around 1.7 million ransomware attacks every single day.

Ransomware Guardian allows users to track daily Victim posts quickly, accurately and easily.
